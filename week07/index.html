About

The Queens School Of Inquiry

Some call it UCLA (the Univeristy Close to LaGuardia Airport!lol), but to many students and teachers, we call it home. A humble 6-12th grade school in the heart of the Worlds Borough, QSI (what we REALLY call it) is home to the finest computer science department in the ENTIRE Kew Gardens Hills area! Our department is second to none, as we almost have a full class set of working 2017-era Chromebook, and an entire computer science teacher (uncertified), who splits his time teaching special ed (Drama), setting up printers and connecting staff cellphones to the school Wifi

Jealous yet? Sometimes I have to PINCH myself to make sure I'm not dreaming.
